FREQUENTLY ASKED

Everything You Wanted To Ask

How much does a business website cost in India?

A professional business website at edokan.in starts at ₹4,999 for a small-business package and goes up to a fully custom e-commerce build. Final pricing depends on the number of pages, design complexity, integrations, and SEO requirements.

How long does it take to build a website?

A standard 5-page business website usually takes 5–10 working days. E-commerce stores typically take 2–4 weeks depending on the number of products and payment integrations.

Do you provide website maintenance and support?

Yes. Every project includes lifetime minor-fix support. We also offer affordable monthly maintenance plans covering updates, backups, security, and content changes.

Will my website rank on Google?

Every website is built with on-page SEO baked in — schema markup, fast performance, clean code, semantic HTML, mobile-first design, and proper meta tags — so it has the strongest possible foundation to rank for your local and business keywords.

Do you help with domain and hosting?

Yes. I help you choose, register, and configure the right domain and hosting. I can also fully manage hosting on your behalf so you never have to worry about technical setup.

Can you build an e-commerce store with online payments?

Absolutely. I build complete e-commerce stores with product management, cart, checkout, Razorpay / Stripe / UPI payments, order tracking, and WhatsApp order alerts.

Are your websites mobile-friendly?

Every website is mobile-first and fully responsive. I test on real phones, not just emulators, so your customers get a flawless experience on any device.

Do you serve clients outside Kolkata?

Yes. While I am based in Kolkata, West Bengal, I serve clients in Dankuni, Chandannagar, Serampore, Hooghly and all over India. The entire process can be handled remotely over WhatsApp, calls, and email.

What if I want to update content myself later?

I can build your website on a content management system like WordPress so you can edit text, images, and prices yourself. I also include a short training session after launch.

How do I get started?

The easiest way is to tap the WhatsApp button or call +91 9038505152. I will discuss your business, goals, and budget, and send you a clear quote within 24 hours.
